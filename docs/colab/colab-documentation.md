CoLab is the SailPoint developer community hub for open-source, community-built identity solutions.

## What you can find in CoLab

CoLab currently includes six categories:

### SaaS Connectors
Pre-built connectors for SaaS platforms.

### Workflows
Automated identity lifecycle workflows.

### Rules
Custom logic and business rules.

### Transforms
Data transformation utilities.

### IIQ Plugins
IdentityIQ platform extensions.

### Community Tools
Developer utilities and community helpers.
